About

Spaces in between

Liminal Zone is a multiplayer virtual reality experience about wandering the places that exist between places — brutalist sanctuaries half-swallowed by sand, endless parks under a quiet sun. There is nothing to win. Just light, architecture, and whoever drifted in with you.

◻

Dreamlike worlds

Hand-built liminal spaces — concrete, dunes, water and trees — designed to feel serene and slightly uncanny.

◎

Drop-in together

No account, no menus, no friction. Put the headset on and you're there, alongside other wanderers.

〰

Proximity voice

Voices carry through space like in real life — approach someone to talk, drift away to be alone.

✧

Mist avatars

Wanderers appear as gentle ghost-like figures of mist, anonymous and calm by design.
